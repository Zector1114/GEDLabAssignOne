---Block Dodger---
A simple game of dodging the falling blocks. If you get hit by one, its game over!

- The system I used a singleton on is an AudioManager as it was recommended one.
- I do think its beneficial as in scenes you'll only really need a single audio manager 99% of the time.

Jump Sound: https://opengameart.org/content/platformer-jumping-sounds

Death Sound: https://opengameart.org/content/bombexplosion8bit
